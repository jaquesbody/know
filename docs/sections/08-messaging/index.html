Know reclaim your digital privacy

What is it? Messaging apps are a gateway to your relationships and your habits — who you call, when you call, where you call from and for how long.

What about privacy? Over 3 billion people use WhatsApp. Although Meta can’t see your messages, they collect a lot of data from your phone which is used to build a digital profile for advertising.

We highly recommend Signal — it feels a lot like WhatsApp, it’s easy to use, they can’t read your messages and collect next to none of your data.

  • Download Signal.
  • Don’t want to broadcast your phone number? Go to Settings > Privacy > Phone number.
  • Run Signal alongside WhatsApp and slowly migrate people over as and when they are ready.
