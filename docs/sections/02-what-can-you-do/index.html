reclaim your digital privacy

Not all privacy advice applies to everyone. What matters is reducing your exposure in the areas that are relevant to your life. You don’t need to do everything in this guide. Start where it makes sense and build from there.

Most data collection happens passively. You don’t hand it over deliberately, it leaks through the tools you use every day. Switch your browser, change to a private DNS, use a different email provider. Small changes compound quickly.

How to use this guide:

  • Work through the sections in order or jump to what’s relevant
  • Tick off items as you go
  • Click Done when you’ve finished a section
  • Don’t rush, small changes that stick are better than doing everything at once
