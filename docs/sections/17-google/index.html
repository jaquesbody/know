Know reclaim your digital privacy

Google is the largest online data collector. They track your location, search history, YouTube views, Android phone usage — they read your emails, text messages, private documents, calendar, photos, what apps you install and a whole lot more. All of it used to build a comprehensive digital profile to target you with ads.

How to limit Google’s reach

  • Check Your Data: Visit Google Takeout to download and review what they know about you.
  • Remove Content: Use Google’s Removal Tool to remove personal info from search results.
  • Check out the alternative apps at privacypack.org.
