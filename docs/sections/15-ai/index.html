reclaim your digital privacy

What is it? Modern AI features (Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI, Claude) are constantly listening, reading and using your data to train their models — scraped from your personal emails, photos, private messages, documents and chat logs. Many of these smart features are on by default, hidden deep in settings, with new capabilities enabled on every software update without your explicit consent.

How to limit AI’s reach

  • Check Settings: Go to every app and device settings. Look for options similar to “Help Improve”, “Personalise”, “AI Features” or “Smart Features” — turn them off.
  • Disable Cloud Processing: Turn off any “Send to Cloud” options for voice assistants and keyboards.
  • Local AI: Install a local model on your device (e.g. Llama3.2:1B) — none of your data is leaked.
  • Private AI: Use Brave’s AI Leo, built into the Brave browser, which is designed to not store your chat history for training.
