reclaim your digital privacy

What is it? We are all familiar with Zoom, Teams, Meet or WhatsApp video calls — they can be great for connecting with family, friends or colleagues.

What about privacy? Although providers often offer an encrypted option, it is sometimes not switched on by default, does not apply to group meetings, or limits the app’s functionality when enabled.

Brave Talk is easy to use, built into the Brave browser, super private and you can screenshare too.

  • Simply visit talk.brave.com.
  • Click the ‘person +’ icon to invite others via a link.
