Know reclaim your digital privacy

  • Install Brave browser (5 mins) Visit brave.com/download. They don’t track your usage, collect data or advertise to you. Turn on Shields to block adverts, cookies and tracking. Give Brave Search and their AI Leo a go too — they don’t store your searches or conversations.

  • Set up a Proton account (10 mins) Visit proton.me then download their password manager Proton Pass. Set super strong passwords, sync them across multiple devices, no need to remember them or copy and paste them.

  • Move from WhatsApp to Signal (5 mins + ongoing) Download from Play Store. Signal collects almost no data whatsoever and has no access to your messages, contacts or call logs. Run Signal alongside WhatsApp and slowly migrate people over as and when.

  • Sign up for a privacy respecting email address (5 mins) Visit proton.me/mail. They don’t read your emails, docs or use your conversations to train their AI models. Stop using your name and DOB in the address.

  • Move to an encrypted cloud storage provider (10 mins) Check out alternatives in the Cloud Storage section. They won’t use your photos or snoop on your documents.

  • Check Have I Been Pwned (30 secs) Visit haveibeenpwned.com. See if your email has been leaked in a data breach. If so, change it and the password immediately.

  • Review your apps (10 mins) Delete any apps you don’t use — they still collect data and drain your battery even when not in use.

  • Review your app permissions (10 mins) Check what permissions you’re giving all your apps. Do they need access to your pictures 24/7? Do they need your exact location, microphone or phone contacts?

  • Check your app settings (30 mins — deliberately difficult) Find the Settings cog in most apps, then dig around for Privacy and Security settings. What is ON? Limit telemetry, switch off advertising settings and check for AI or ‘smart’ features — these can scrape anything to feed AI models.

  • Check out PrivacyPack (5 mins) Visit privacypack.org. Brilliant alternatives to the standard Google, Meta and Microsoft products.

  • Add a 3-month reminder to your calendar (2 mins) Add a recurring reminder every 3 months to: delete unused apps, review app permissions and settings, check Have I Been Pwned, unsubscribe from unwanted emails and change any compromised passwords.
