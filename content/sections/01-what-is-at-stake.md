Your digital profile isn't just your search history. It includes your passwords, intimate emails, contacts, location history, the metadata of your daily life (dates, times, durations) and a whole lot more. Your data can be sold to brokers and collected by governments, used to surveil you, exposed in breaches and used to sell to you.

**You should control what personal data you share and who you share it with.**
